Location Data (geo-tracking):

Zeencrush has for main purpose and vocation to allow Members to find the people near them by use of location. The Member will be asked for permission to collect and process his/her geo-tracking in order to allow Zeencrush to identify the nearby Members that accepted the geo-tracking. The news feed is created by these near geo-location pointers which permits to propose to the Member only profiles that he’s nearby with and that fits his/her criteria. Zeencrush may, in case of express consent, access the Member's position, approximately or more precisely depending on which technology is being used.

The Member is free to withdraw his/her consent at any time, and deactivate the geo-tracking technology on his smartphone. By doing that, the Member’s news feed is not updated anymore and the member cannot see the other Members he /she is nearby with after the deactivation of the geo-tracking technology.

Previous Conversations:

ZEENCRUSH doesn’t allow previous chats to be stored in any format and thus doesn’t allow chats to be shown after a certain period of time. The users shall never complain about chats not being shown or deleted.
